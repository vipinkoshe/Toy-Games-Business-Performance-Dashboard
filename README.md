# 🧸 Toy & Games Business Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Toy & Games Business Performance Dashboard** is an interactive **Microsoft Excel Dashboard** designed to analyze sales, profitability, inventory, and product performance. Built using Excel's data analysis and visualization features, the dashboard provides a clear overview of business performance through dynamic charts, KPIs, and interactive filters.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor overall business performance.
* Track revenue, profit, and profit margin.
* Analyze monthly sales trends.
* Identify top-performing products.
* Monitor low-stock inventory.
* Compare revenue across product categories and store locations.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Interactive Filters

* Store City
* Month
* Store Location
* Product Category

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** ₹14.44M
* **Total Profit:** ₹4.01M
* **Profit Margin:** 28%
* **Total Units Sold:** 109,565
* **Total Stock:** 29,742

### 🔹 Dashboard Visualizations

* 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend
* 📊 Top 5 Products by Revenue
* 📦 Low Stock Products
* 🚨 Actionable Low Stock Alert
* 📊 Revenue by Product Category
* 🍩 Revenue by Store Location

## 🛠️ Tools & Features Used

* Microsoft Excel

## 📈 Key Insights

* Toys generate the highest revenue among all product categories.
* Monthly revenue trends help identify seasonal sales patterns.
* Low-stock alerts enable timely inventory replenishment.
* Top-performing products can be identified for better sales planning.
* Revenue distribution across store locations supports location-based business decisions.


## 🚀 Conclusion

This Excel dashboard transforms raw sales and inventory data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking. It serves as an effective decision-support tool for monitoring business performance, inventory management, and sales analysis.
